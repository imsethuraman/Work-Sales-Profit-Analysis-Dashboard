# Work-Sales-Profit-Analysis-Dashboard
A comprehensive Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard built using Excel (Advanced) to analyze business performance across time, products, customers, and geography.

🚀 Project Overview

Track sales, revenue, and profit performance
Identify top-performing products & customers
Analyze profit trends over time
Understand regional and demographic contributions
Enable data-driven decision making

🎯 Key Business Questions Answered

Which products generate the highest profit?
Who are the top customers driving revenue?
How does profit vary monthly, quarterly, and yearly?
Which countries contribute most to the overall profit?
What is the impact of product pricing and categories?
How do age groups and gender influence profit?
